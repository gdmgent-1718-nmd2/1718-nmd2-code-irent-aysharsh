How Big Friendly Shoulders came together

BFS was founded after Lukas and I met at Pukkelpop. I, an enthousiastic fan, was trying to catch a glimpse of Bruno Mars. Unfortunately being very short is no advantage at a big festival.
A whole new world had opened up for me. Festivals had changed forever. Nevertheless, sharing is caring. So Lukas’ shoulders are available for everyone who wishes.
Luckily I met just the right guy, this Big Friendly Giant was as kind to offer me a shoulder ride. Once he’d risen me up in the sky, I could see the whole of the stage and see Bruno’s full performance.
